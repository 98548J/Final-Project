My Story

Prompt

We strive to create a rich and varied educational environment through admitting students with a wide range of:


  • Goals
  • Interests
  • Skills and talents
  • Life experiences
  • Perspectives
  • Cultures

Tell us your story. What will you contribute to our university community? Be specific.


Essay

As students meander towards their next class, the sound of chatter skips aimlessly through the hallway of our school. Suddenly, the sound trips -- completely face-planting. I tripped the chatter with a shout: "Hey, Hunter!" "Hey, Caleb!" Hunter exclaims back. We both grin as we clap our hands together. Did one of us win the lottery, a free cruise? Nope, I just wanted to say hi.


The reason I do this is simple: The goal of making my friends smile; if that requires making a fool of myself in the hallway, so be it. Further, I want my friends to feel genuinely happy when they see me, so I try to be a real friend: being honest with them, listening to them, encouraging them, and serving them. In turn, they reward me with a grin.


Not even my teachers are exempt from this treatment: during lunch today, my calculus teacher and I tried to see if a summation formula we had made during class matched Gauss' summation formula. Similarly, my love of engineering has sparked some fun after-class conversations with my physics teacher -- have you ever wanted to know the inertia of a flywheel with some holes in it?


I also have a love for music. Through music, I have had many opportunities to serve (and entertain) my peers by playing my saxophone in Christmas programs, dinner dances, and talent shows.


It's my outgoing nature and love of math, physics, engineering, and music that I will use to contribute to my community.


I look down the hall and see someone else I know.


I grin -- perfect.
